簡單使用守則 · Quick Tips

📐
手機鏡頭盡量同張卡平行，唔好斜影，否則會影響準確度。
Keep your phone parallel to the card. Tilt reduces accuracy.
📄
張卡要平放，唔好彎曲、拱起或凹凸不平。
Keep the card flat. Bent or curved cards affect the measurement.
📷
盡量用高解像度、清晰、光線平均嘅相。
Use a sharp, high-resolution photo with even lighting.
🔭
建議用 1.5× 或 2× 鏡頭；避免 0.5× / 超廣角，減少魚眼或桶形變形。
Use the 1.5× or 2× lens if available. Avoid 0.5× / ultra-wide — it causes fisheye / barrel distortion at card edges.

Apply the Invert / Invert HC filter on the back: white spots, dust, and surface marks turn dark — much easier to spot.

Photo tilt severity
—
Compares opposite outer-quad edges. High tilt = perspective distortion present; small handle errors translate into larger mm errors. Use 2× / telephoto and retake for accuracy.
Edge curvature (Refine mode)
—
Worst-side perpendicular residual of the 4 sample points from the fitted line. High = edges visibly bowed (lens distortion, bent card, or placement error). Retake with 2× / telephoto if > 0.25 mm.

Handle placement guide
The colored frame has two visible edges. Place yellow at the inner one — where the central artwork actually starts — not the outer edge of the colored band.
PSA centering thresholds
  • Gem Mint 10: ≤ 55/45 front, ≤ 75/25 back
  • Mint 9: ≤ 60/40 front, ≤ 90/10 back
  • NM-MT 8: ≤ 65/35 front
  • NM 7: ≤ 70/30 front
Tips & caveats
  • Outer handles = physical card edge. Inner handles = where the central artwork begins (inner edge of the colored band).
  • If Σ L+R or Σ T+B looks far from your caliper-measured total, your handle placement (most likely inner) is biased — re-check.
  • Full-art / V / ex / SAR cards often lack a clear inner frame — centering becomes ambiguous.
  • Card is assumed flat. Bent cards give wrong mm offsets.
  • Card dimensions assumed 63 × 88 mm.
  • Best capture: dark matte background, no flash, even diffused light, phone parallel to card, full card with margin, sleeves removed. Lens choice matters: prefer 2× / telephoto if your phone has one. Ultra-wide adds barrel distortion that bends straight card edges and biases L/R borders ~0.1-0.2 mm.
  • Handles auto-lock after 1.5 s of no movement to prevent the tiny release-nudge. A countdown ring in the center of the canvas shows remaining hold time. Lift and tap again to resume; green ring = locked.
