PSA centering thresholds
  • Gem Mint 10: ≤ 55/45 front, ≤ 75/25 back
  • Mint 9: ≤ 60/40 front, ≤ 90/10 back
  • NM-MT 8: ≤ 65/35 front
  • NM 7: ≤ 70/30 front
Tips & caveats
  • Outer = physical card edge. Inner = inner colored frame edge (yellow/holo border).
  • Full-art / V / ex / SAR cards often lack a clear inner frame — centering becomes ambiguous.
  • Card is assumed flat. Bent cards will give wrong mm offsets.
  • Card dimensions assumed 63 × 88 mm. mm values are estimates derived from the homography.
  • For best capture: dark matte background, no flash, even diffused light, phone parallel to card, full card in frame with margin, sleeves removed.
