Handle placement guide
The colored frame has two visible edges. Place yellow at the inner one — where the central artwork actually starts — not the outer edge of the colored band.
PSA centering thresholds
  • Gem Mint 10: ≤ 55/45 front, ≤ 75/25 back
  • Mint 9: ≤ 60/40 front, ≤ 90/10 back
  • NM-MT 8: ≤ 65/35 front
  • NM 7: ≤ 70/30 front
Tips & caveats
  • Cyan = physical card edge. Yellow = where the central artwork begins (inner edge of the colored band).
  • If Σ L+R or Σ T+B looks far from your caliper-measured total, your handle placement (most likely yellow) is biased — re-check.
  • Full-art / V / ex / SAR cards often lack a clear inner frame — centering becomes ambiguous.
  • Card is assumed flat. Bent cards give wrong mm offsets.
  • Card dimensions assumed 63 × 88 mm.
  • Best capture: dark matte background, no flash, even diffused light, phone parallel to card, full card with margin, sleeves removed, use main lens not ultra-wide.
